Welcome to Sarinda Sobari Resort

Located in the serene heart of nature, Sarinda Sobari Resort blends traditional elegance with eco-modern living. Our resort offers a tranquil escape where birdsong replaces your alarm clock and every sunset paints the sky with magic.

Enjoy lakefront views, treehouse cabins, wellness spas, and gourmet local dining. Whether it’s a honeymoon, solo recharge, or family vacation — we offer the space, service, and soul you seek.
